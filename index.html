It is a popular destination for nature lovers and weekend getaways.
Its rocky hills and natural beauty make it an ideal escape from city life.

NATURE PARK

Open Time : 7.00 AM - 8.00 PM
Cost : Rs.15/- for adults and Rs.10/- for children.
Place : Punganoor

The park is the best destination for all ages of people and provide a feeling of relaxation and stunning environment. It consists of diversified and enormous numbers of flora. There are number of things such as artificial cascade or the waterfall, children's park, musical fountain with multi-coloured lighting, fish aquarium, seasonal garden, bamboo house, poly house and many more to engage kids and even elders for a long time.

VELAVAN TEMPLE

Open Time : 06.00 AM - 8.00 PM
Place : Nilovoor

Click here for Location...


Velavan Temple is dedicated to worshipping Lord Murugan. Thetemple has a fantastic view of the surrounding hills and it houses a rare Gadothgajan, the son of Bheema, at its entrance.

Yelagiri's most popular hill and one of the most breath taking as well, the Swami Malai Hill is the highest point of the hill station. A 3 km trek from the Punganoor lake will take you to the hill top.

SHIVAN TEMPLE

Open Time : 07.00 AM - 1.00 PM And 3.00 PM - 7.00PM
Place :Mangalam

Click here for Location...


Shiva temple is located at the foothills of Swamimalai, on Mangalam Road. Recently built temple has a Circular Garbha griha. Statues of Maharishis adorn the front yard. The Temple offers beautiful views of hills and valleys all around

PERUMAL TEMPLE

Open Time : 07.30 AM - 12.00 PM And 4.00 PM - 7.00PM
Place : Athanvoor

Click here for Location...


The temple has a few more shrines including Ramanujar, Manavala Mamunigal and Anjaneyar.It is said an ancient temple existed here centuries ago, and an ancient deity of Adi Kesava Perumal along with some lamps were excavated here while constructing the current temple.

NACHI AMMAN

Open Time : 07.00 AM - 1.00 PM And 3.00 PM - 7.00PM
Place : Nilavoor

Click here for Location...


Kadavu Nachi Amman temple, a devi temple situated on the banks of Nilavoor lake is a calm, serene place. One can walk to Nilavoor Cliff view point from here. The temple with a back drop of green hills around is quite scenic.
